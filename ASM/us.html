ABOUT TONG GIANG PRODUCTION AND TRADING COMPANY LIMITED

Tong Giang Bakery is a leading traditional and modern bakery brand in Ho Chi Minh City. With more than a decade of construction and development, Tong Giang Bakery has become a trusted choice for cake lovers in Vietnam. Established in 2012 in District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, the company has constantly innovated and created to bring hundreds of thousands of high-quality cakes to customers.

ESTABLISHMENT

Mr. Ha Truong Giang, a person who is passionate about baking and has experience learning from Asian and European cuisines, officially established Tong Giang Bakery on September 15, 2012. With the goal of bringing unique, sophisticated and safe cakes to Vietnamese people, he built the brand from scratch. Within just the first 3 years of operation, Tong Giang Bakery has expanded with 5 branches in Ho Chi Minh City and quickly became a prestigious cake brand. The company's development comes not only from product quality but also from dedicated and thoughtful customer service.

MISSION

Tong Giang Bakery is committed to bringing cakes made from natural ingredients, imported directly from Europe. Our products are processed according to modern processes, combining traditional style and innovative innovation, ensuring absolute satisfaction for customers.

BUSINESS COMMITMENT

Our company prioritizes integrity, serving customers with dedication, creativity, and continuous innovation.

ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

Tong Giang Production and Trading Company Limited operates with a compact and efficient organizational model. The staff includes: Production department: Experienced bakers. Business department: Specializing in supporting customers and expanding the market. Creative department: Responsible for developing new product lines. All departments work together to meet the increasing needs of customers.

FOCUS ON HUMAN DEVELOPMENT

Tong Giang Bakery always puts the human factor first. The staff is professionally trained, constantly improving their skills and communication skills. We regularly organize workshops and seminars to update new trends in the bakery industry. In addition, the consulting staff is always ready to support customers through online platforms and at stores, providing fast and professional service.

REPUTATION AND QUALITY

Tong Giang Bakery's motto is: "Quality is the foundation of trust". We are proud to bring cakes that are not only beautiful but also delicious and nutritious. All products go through a strict quality control process before reaching customers.

ALWAYS LISTENING

Every feedback from our customers motivates us to improve our services and products daily. Feel free to share your thoughts with us!
